# Questions

This is a table of contents for all the questions we have you show the questions and you have

## Create a new question

To create a new question, click on the `Add question` button. You will be asked to provide a question and a doc group for this specific question.

![](/images/questions/create.png)

One you provide the question and the doc group, click on the `Submit` button. you can show the question in the list of questions.

## Edit a question

To edit a question, click on the `Edit` button. You will be asked to provide a question and a doc group for this specific question.

![](/images/questions/update.png)

## Delete a question

To delete a question, click on the `Delete` button. You will be asked to confirm the deletion. Once you confirm, the question will be deleted and you will no longer be able to access it.

![](/images/questions/delete.png)
