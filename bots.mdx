# Bot management

Here you can manage your bots. You can create a new bot, edit an existing bot, or delete a bot.
![](/images/bot/bot-page.png)


## Create a new bot

To create a new bot, click on the `Create Bot` button. You will be asked to provide a name for the bot a description and an API key also if you want bot active or not lastly you asked about what is the knowledge base for your bot. Once you provide the bot information , click on the `Create` button. Your bot will be created and you will be show the new bot in bots page.
![](/images/bot/create.png)


## Edit a bot

To edit a bot, click on the `Edit` button. You will be able to edit the bot name, description, API key, and knowledge base. Once you have made the changes, click on the `Update` button. Your bot will be updated with the new information.
![](/images/bot/update.png)


## Delete a bot

To delete a bot, click on the `Delete` button. You will be asked to confirm the deletion. Once you confirm, the bot will be deleted and you will no longer be able to access it.
![](/images/bot/delete.png)
